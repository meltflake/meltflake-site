Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how meltflake ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with our applications and services ("Apps"). We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Our Apps are designed with privacy in mind. Most of our Apps process data entirely on your device and do not collect, transmit, or store personal information on external servers.

Depending on the specific App, we may collect:

  • No personal data — Many of our Apps operate fully offline and collect nothing.
  • Anonymous usage analytics — Some Apps may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (e.g., crash reports, feature usage counts) to improve the product. This data cannot be used to identify you.
  • Purchase information — If you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not receive or store your payment details.

2. How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve and maintain our Apps
  • Fix bugs and resolve technical issues
  • Understand general usage patterns

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data stays on your device. Our Apps are designed to process files and information locally. We do not operate servers that store your personal data or files.

If an App offers optional cloud sync or backup features, this will be clearly disclosed within the App, and you will have full control over whether to enable it.

4. Third-Party Services

Our Apps may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — for app distribution and in-app purchases
  • Apple / Google crash reporting — for anonymous crash diagnostics (if enabled by the platform)

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data
  • Opt out of data collection
  • Request a copy of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Since most of our Apps do not collect personal data, these rights may not be applicable. If you have questions, please contact us.

7. International Users

Our Apps are available worldwide. If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or other regions with data protection laws, we process any data in accordance with applicable regulations including GDPR.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our Apps after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
